Cactus belongs to the dicotyledonous plant family Cactaceae and has a tree-like axis. It is a perennial plant with thickened succulent fleshy stems covered with prickles, hairs or bristles. Fruits of most cacti are fleshy, berry-shaped, many of them even edible (Cereus, Opuntia).

Cacti love bright light and fresh air, so a sunny side will be ideal. Create a simulated desert environment for your cacti. Water infrequently but abundantly. The soil should dry out well between waterings.

Cacti attract flower growers with their peculiarities and originality. But all these qualities bring certain difficulties when growing cacti at home.

Plant cacti in pots with removable bottoms that are 15% higher than the root system of the plant. The risk of cactus root rot will be minimized in taller and less wide pots.
